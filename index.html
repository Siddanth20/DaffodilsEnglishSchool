About Us

Daffodils English school was first started as Saraswathi Vidyashala in
a house at Sanjaynagar in 1978 with nine students, Ms Sequeira as a
teacher and Ms. Gowrama as the help. The school was shifted to the
present location in 1979.Today, Saraswathi Vidyashala, renamed as
Daffodils English School in June 1983, stands on a two-acre plot, an
edifice that symbolises the determination and hard work that our
Late Mentor and his family have put in to make the school, one of the
best in Bengaluru.
